Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint or 전지적 독자 시점

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint, written by the duo SingShong tells the story of a 28 year old businessman, Kim Dokja, that read a novel called "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World" over the span of a decade. One fateful day, the real world becomes the apocalyptic world of the novel. Kim Dokja tries to figure out why the world became this way and also how to survive using his extensive knowledge of the novel.

Why do I like it?

  • All the characters are well written
  • Kim Dokja is very relatable
  • Balances action and emotional parts very well
  • I cried while reading this so obviously I liked it
